# Privacy Policy — Side A

**Last updated:** June 2026  
**Developer:** Sight-A  
**Contact:** sight.a@icloud.com

---

## 1. Overview

Side A stores all data exclusively on your device. No personal data is transmitted to us or any third parties.

---

## 2. Data Collection

Side A does not collect or store any personal data. All content (albums, tracks, categories, cover photos, notes) is stored exclusively in the local database on your iPhone.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

### MusicBrainz
When searching for albums by barcode or catalog number, requests are sent to the MusicBrainz API. Only the search term (barcode or catalog number) is transmitted. No personal data is shared.  
Privacy Policy: https://metabrainz.org/privacy

### Discogs
If you enter a personal Discogs token in the settings, search requests are sent to the Discogs API. The token is stored exclusively on your device. No further personal data is transmitted.  
Privacy Policy: https://www.discogs.com/privacy

### Apple Contacts
When recording lent albums, you can optionally select a contact from your address book. Access is handled via Apple's system contact picker — Side A does not have direct access to your address book. Only the selected name is stored locally.

---

## 4. Backup & Export

The export function creates a JSON file containing your album data. This file only leaves your device if you actively share it via the share sheet. Side A has no access to exported files.

---

## 5. Changes

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy as needed. The current version is always linked from the Side A App Store page.
